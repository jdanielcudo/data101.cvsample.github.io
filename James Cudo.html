Background

I am an undergraduate student at the College of Science at De La Salle University, Manila, Philippines. Currently, I am working as a researcher in the DLSU Practical Genomics Laboratory (PGL). For my underegraduate thesis, I am interrogating gene structure and variation in an immune response gene called Toll-like receptor 4 (TLR4) in Philippine livestock populations under the supervision of Ma. Carmen Ablan-Lagman, Ph.D.

Passionate about the intersection of data science and molecular biology, I am deeply interested in harnessing the power of advanced analytics and machine learning algorithms to unravel complex biological systems, identify meaningful patterns in high-throughput genomic data, and drive groundbreaking discoveries in the field of molecular biology research.
